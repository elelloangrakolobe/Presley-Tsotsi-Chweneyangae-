About Presley Chweneyagae

Home About Achievements Videos Pictures

Presley Chweneyagae was born in 1984 in South Africa. He became internationally known for his role in Tsotsi (2005), which won the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film. Raised by a single mother, Presley discovered acting through youth theater. His passion led him from local stage plays to global film screens.

Beyond acting, Presley works with youth empowerment and social justice initiatives in South Africa. His performances reflect real-life issues, especially for young people in townships.
